Accounting Standard
ASC 946 — Financial Services: Investment Companies.

Under ASC 946, all investments are carried at fair value. Changes in FV flow through the income statement — not OCI — and are allocated to partners' capital.
Model Notes
Distributions and ROC are shown as separate rows to illustrate the classification difference. In many fund reports, ROC is a component within the distributions line. This model excludes management fees and fund expenses for clarity — a clean-case illustration only.
